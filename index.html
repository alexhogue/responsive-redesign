The Grand Adventure

What We Do

The Grand Adventure is a non-profit organization with the mission of Healing Through Exploration. We strive to help those in and recovering from long-term treatment for cancer and hematology diagnosis to accomplish this in two ways, through both in-person and virtual format program opportunities.

What Makes Us Unique

Customized Experiences

Our programming is carefully crafted with input from patient's healthcare providers, naturalist guides, and our experienced program team.

Financial Access

Our programs are 100% free, making them accessible to a hospital's entire patient base, regardless of socio-economic background.

Ability Access

We offer in-person and virtual programs that bring the power of healing through exploration to your patients, regardless of geography or physical ability.

Reliable Care

Our programs are designed in concert with hospitals to deliver flexible, best-fit programming and tight HIPAA compliance.
